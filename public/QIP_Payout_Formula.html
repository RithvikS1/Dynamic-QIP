QIP Payout Formula

Fully formatted equation and definitions for distribution of funds across clinics.

Tip: In the print dialog, choose “Save as PDF”.

Final Payout

\[ P_i = \left( \frac{V_i^{\gamma} \, PF_i}{\sum_j V_j^{\gamma} \, PF_j} \right) P_T (1-\beta) \;+\; \left( \frac{I_i}{\sum_j I_j} \right) P_T \, \beta \]

  • Main Pool Component: \( \left( \frac{V_i^{\gamma} PF_i}{\sum_j V_j^{\gamma} PF_j} \right) P_T (1-\beta) \)
  • Bonus Pool Component: \( \left( \frac{I_i}{\sum_j I_j} \right) P_T \, \beta \)

Components

Base Share and Normalization

\[ B_i = \frac{V_i^{\gamma}}{\sum_j V_j^{\gamma}} \quad ; \quad AS_i = B_i \cdot PF_i \quad ; \quad NAS_i = \frac{AS_i}{\sum_j AS_j} \]

Performance Factor per Benchmark

\[ PF_{i,k} = 1 + \alpha \cdot \max\!\left(0,\; \frac{R_{i,k} - R_{b,k}}{R_{b,k}}\right) \]

Combined Performance Factor (Clinic \(i\))

Weights \(w_k\) are enforced to sum to 1 in the UI; otherwise simple average.

\[ PF_i = \sum_k w_k \, PF_{i,k} \quad \text{(weighted; } \sum_k w_k = 1\text{)} \]

\( PF_i = \text{average of } PF_{i,k} \text{ over } k \quad \text{(no weights)} \)

Improvement per Benchmark and Total Improvement

\[ Improvement_{i,k} = \max\!\left(0,\; \frac{R_{i,k} - R_{\\text{prev},i,k}}{R_{\\text{prev},i,k}}\right) \]

Improvement is weighted by benchmark priorities, similar to performance factors.

\[ I_i = \sum_k w_k \, Improvement_{i,k} \quad \text{(weighted; } \sum_k w_k = 1\text{)} \]

\( I_i = \text{average of } Improvement_{i,k} \text{ over } k \quad \text{(no weights)} \)

Main and Bonus Payouts

\[ Payout_{\\text{main},i} = NAS_i \\cdot P_T \\cdot (1-\\beta) \\quad ; \\quad Bonus_i = \\left( \\frac{I_i}{\\sum_j I_j} \\right) P_T \\cdot \\beta \\]

Variables and Symbols

  • \(P_i\): Final payout for clinic \(i\)
  • \(Payout_{\\text{main},i}\): Main pool payout for clinic \(i\)
  • \(Bonus_i\): Bonus payout for clinic \(i\)
  • \(V_i\): Clinic \(i\) volume (e.g., number of patients)
  • \(R_{i,k}\): Clinic \(i\)'s current rate on benchmark \(k\)
  • \(R_{b,k}\): Benchmark target rate for benchmark \(k\)
  • \(R_{\\text{prev},i,k}\): Clinic \(i\)'s previous rate on benchmark \(k\)
  • \(w_k\): Weight for benchmark \(k\) (optional; if none provided, all benchmarks weighted equally)
  • \(PF_{i,k}\): Performance factor for clinic \(i\) on benchmark \(k\)
  • \(PF_i\): Combined performance factor for clinic \(i\) (weighted or simple average of \(PF_{i,k}\))
  • \(B_i\): Base share for clinic \(i\) from volume weighting
  • \(AS_i\): Adjusted share for clinic \(i\) prior to normalization
  • \(NAS_i\): Normalized adjusted share for clinic \(i\)
  • \(I_i\): Total improvement score for clinic \(i\)
  • \(P_T\): Total payout pool
  • \(\\beta\): Bonus percentage (fraction in \([0,1]\))
  • \(\\alpha\): Performance sensitivity parameter
  • \(\\gamma\): Volume weighting parameter
